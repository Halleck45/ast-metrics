Test Quality

How effectively do your tests exercise your application code?
This page evaluates two dimensions: Isolation (does each test focus on a limited set of application structures?) and Traceability (is every application structure covered by at least one test?). Well-isolated tests are easier to maintain, faster to diagnose when they fail, and less likely to break due to unrelated changes.
